Mario League

Mario League is a gaming event for all to come along and play a range of games on a range of consoles and PCs. Some of the Games that will be available are: Mario Kart 8, Rocket League, and a variety of games for the PC. ~ Including Emulators for other consoles.

Players donate to be able to play, all donations go to CLIC Sargent - Young Lives vs Cancer. Donations will have a minimum requirement of 50p for 10 minutes of your chosen game. There will also be a Mario Kart 8 gaming Tournament £1 to enter, and could win a £10 game voucher

The event will take place at Kilwinning Campus on the 9th of May from 12-2pm in the Lecture Theatre 5, There will be an information booth on the main entrance for any equires on the day.
